About Us

About Pragathi Rural Development Trust

Pragathi Rural Development Trust, a Non-Government Organization, based in Krishnapuram Village, Pakala Mandal, Tirupathi District, Andhra Pradesh State, India stands as a beacon of hope and compassion.

The organisation works for ‘the cause and care of the disadvantaged, marginalized, and vulnerable rural people to improve their quality of life’. It runs sanitation, infection prevention, nutrition, healthcare, aged care, organic agriculture, water harvesting, non-formal adult education, self-employment, cultural and awareness initiatives.

Pragathi Rural Development Trust advocates strongly for

Research on Hospital Acquired Infections (HAI) / Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR).

Research on prevention of cancer cervix in rural women.

Prevention of diet related health illnesses in rural areas through Nutrition Programs / Wellness Centres.

Pragathi Rural Development Trust is committed to fostering health, happiness, peace, harmony, and empowerment for the underprivileged. Through our diverse social welfare initiatives, we strive to create a sustainable and inclusive society where every individual can thrive with dignity and hope.
