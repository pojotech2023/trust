About Us

About Pragathi Rural Development Truth

At Pragathi Rural Development Truth, our comprehensive range of welfare services encompasses various aspects of social welfare, aimed at fostering a better quality of life for all. We extend a helping hand through:

Social security services

Offering assistance and support to the elderly and disabled individuals who are often overlooked by society.

Rehabilitation services

Guiding individuals towards rehabilitation and reintegration into society after facing adversity.

Medical Services

Ensuring access to essential healthcare services for those who cannot afford it, promoting better health outcomes for all.

Social Services

Addressing the diverse social needs of the community, fostering a sense of belonging and support.

Family and child welfare services

Strengthening families and safeguarding the well-being of children, the most vulnerable members of society.

Employment advisory services

Empowering young people with the guidance and resources needed to embark on a fulfilling career path.

Our welfare initiatives are not merely about providing temporary relief but are designed to be holistic and sustainable. We believe in a proactive approach that encompasses preventive, nurturing, supportive, and remedial measures. By identifying and prioritizing the most pressing needs of the community, we strive to allocate our resources efficiently, ensuring that they reach those who need them the most. Our ultimate aim is to facilitate the transition of individuals from dependency to self-reliance, empowering them to lead dignified and fulfilling lives.

Pragathi Rural Development Truth is committed to fostering peace, harmony, and empowerment for the underprivileged. Through our diverse social welfare initiatives, we strive to create a sustainable and inclusive society where every individual can thrive with dignity and hope.
